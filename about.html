THE MIND.

I am a technologist and engineer studying at Sagar Institute of Research & Technology (SIRT), Bhopal. My work focuses on building tools that merge rigorous mathematical backend systems with responsive, tactile user interfaces.

At SIRT Bhopal, I found my footing at the intersection of classical software engineering and low-level computer architecture. My curiosity naturally expanded into ethical hacking—analyzing security boundaries, tracing protocols, and understanding how data leaks or gets compromised. This perspective informs my code, making me design systems that are not just elegant but defensive and robust.

However, pure logic without form is incomplete. Creative technology is my vehicle for expression. I write custom fragment shaders, build real-time interactive particle simulations, and leverage generative models to bridge the gap between machine computations and human sensory experience. I believe that engineering is a creative discipline.

Whether deploying deep learning models on serverless clusters, constructing optimized WebGL graphics loops, or auditing codebases for architectural vulnerabilities, I strive for absolute simplicity, speed, and design integrity.
